Novaline TMS

In Development

An internal transport management system designed around the workflows we use daily. Tracks jobs from quote to invoice, manages clients, vehicles, drivers, and subcontractors in one place.

Built to reduce admin overhead and keep operations organised.

  • Client and job management
  • Vehicle and driver records
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Quotes and invoicing
  • Status tracking

Driver Portal

Coming Soon

A simple interface for drivers to view job offers, accept or decline work, and access job details. Designed to reduce phone calls and speed up job allocation.

Connects directly to the TMS for real-time updates.

  • View offered jobs
  • Accept or decline
  • Job details and instructions
  • Completion confirmation
  • Document upload (planned)

Client Portal

Coming Soon

Client-facing access to view live jobs, download proof of delivery documents, see invoices, and receive status updates. Read-only access to start, with more features planned.

Reduces enquiries and improves transparency.

  • Live job tracking
  • POD downloads
  • Invoice access
  • Status notifications
